Soli-event for Greek Artists
in Ernst Busch

Coming very soon!

This is a soli-event, against the recent precidential decree 85/2022, that the Greek Government has applied. With this decree, all artistical diplomas, from state or private schools, are equated to high-school degrees.

We gather to make some art, discuss and get informed. There are gonna be Comedy Shows, Concerts, Discussions, Movie Projections and more. Exact program will be announced soon. This serious subject has deeply affected Greek society since 3 months now. National theatres, national drama schools, national dance schools are occupied from their students, proffessors have resigned, all the artistic community are on the streets, striking and demanding: -Recognized University Level Studies/Diplomas, as defined by the Greek Constitution -Institutionalized Wage/Professional Framework We need your support Solidarity with Greek Artists.
